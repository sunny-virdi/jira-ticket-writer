# jira-ticket-writer
Turn raw notes into structured JIRA tickets in seconds.

## The User
Product managers who waste time formatting messy notes, 
Slack threads, and meeting scribbles into proper JIRA tickets.

## The Problem
Writing a good JIRA ticket takes 5–10 minutes of reformatting 
work that adds no value. Teams do this dozens of times a week.

## The Solution
Paste your raw notes. Get a structured ticket with:
- Summary (one line)
- Description (context + background)
- Acceptance Criteria (testable conditions)
- Story Points (estimated)

Ready to copy into JIRA in 30 seconds.

## Success Metric
50 tickets generated by real users in the first 2 weeks.
